Sealings

Members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints believe in being married for time and all eternity which is only possible through the power Sealing Power of the Jesus Christ authority given to worthy priesthood holders.

Baptisms for the Dead

Members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints believe that baptism is a saving ordinance needed to live with our Heavenly Father again. Because of this we practice baptism for the dead, where members are baptized proxy for their family members that have passed away.

Visitor Center

Many of our beautiful temples have visitor centers where all are welcome to come in and learn about temples, family history, and the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Check out our temples page and see if there is visitor center and the temple you are visiting.
